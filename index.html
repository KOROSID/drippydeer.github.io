About Us

Drippy Deer is a collection of 888 deer NFTs-unique digital collectibles stored on the Polygon blockchain. There are no bonding curves here. Buying a Drippy Deer costs 0.007 ETH. (Fair Launch, Fair distribution: All deer cost 0.007 ETH) Note: 20 deer are being withheld from the sale. (This will be used for giveaways, puzzle rewards, to get early access to join our discord server ) Each Drippy Deer is unique and programmatically generated from various possible traits, including expressions, headwear, clothing, and more. All the deer are drippy. When you buy a drippy deer, you’re not simply buying an avatar or a rare piece of art. You are gaining membership access to the club whose benefits and offerings will increase over time. Your Drippy Deer can serve as your digital identity, and open digital doors for you. Ownership and commercial usage are given rights given to the consumer over their NFT.
